column names¶

'Player': Player name
'Pos': Position
'Age': Age
'Tm':Team
'G': Games
'GS':Games Started
'MP':Minutes played
'FG':Field Goals(2P+3P) 'FGA': FG Attempts
'FG%':FG percentage
'3P':3P FG
'3PA':3P Attempts
'3P%': 3P Percentage
'2P': 2P FG
'2PA': 2P Attempts
'2P%': 2P Percentage
'eFG%': Effective field goal percentage ((2P+(3P*1.5))/FGA)
'FT': Free throw
'FTA': Free throw attempts
'FT%': Free throw percentages
'ORB': Offensive rebounds
'DRB': Deffensive rebounds
'TRB': Total rebounds
'AST': Assists
'STL': Steals
'BLK': Blocks
'TOV': Turnovers 'PF': Personal Fouls 'PTS': Points
